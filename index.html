I'm a digital marketing manager specializing in SEO, paid advertising, and content optimization. My goal is to help businesses grow by increasing their online visibility, attracting high-quality traffic, and improving conversions.

I work with website SEO optimization, including keyword research, on-page structure (Title, Meta, H1–H3), content improvement, and internal linking strategies. I also continuously analyze user behavior to understand what performs best and identify opportunities for improvement.

I create and manage advertising campaigns, primarily using Meta Ads, where I test different audiences and creative approaches while optimizing performance based on data. In addition, I implement email marketing solutions from newsletters to automated flows focused on engagement and sales.

My decisions are data-driven, using tools like Google Analytics and Search Console. I analyze performance, identify growth opportunities, and continuously optimize processes to achieve measurable results.
